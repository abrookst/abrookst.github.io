This game was made for the 2023 SSJN 2023! Which had a focus on a heavy narrative. While this project took a lot out of me, since I was working a full time job at Firaxis while writing and programming this game entirely by myself. But I am proud of quite a few things, one of those being the aesthetic. I was heavily inspired by games like Cult of the Lamb, Hotline Miami, Control, and Neon White. Additionally, I wanted to give all of the characters distinct voices, but without having to hire/scout full on voice actors. So I decided to go with an Animal Crossing style garbled gibbirish. There was already a library for this for Renpy! But it had not ben updated to the latest version of Renpy/Python 3. So I forked the original, and created a new updated version, you can check that out here
